Who Am I?

I'm Tucker. I'm a 26-year-old from Boulder, Colorado. I created this website to display my web programming skills. I enjoy good company, food, craft beer, mountians, books, and everything relating to the internet. This site is where I display my favorite work.

My Background

I grew up in Colorado in the shadow of the Rocky Mountains. For college I studied political science at Eckerd College in St. Petersburg, Florida.

Upon graduating I became captivated by the creativity and challenge of web development. I spent the next year traveling in Southeast Asia and Europe while building websites for clients.

After leveling up these skills at the intensive full-stack bootcamp Galvanize, I am back on the road and seeking web opportunities of all kinds.
