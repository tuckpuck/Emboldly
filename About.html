Who Am I?

I'm Tucker. I'm a 24-year-old who lives in Boulder, Colorado. I enjoy delicious food, craft beer, books, and the internet. I am also really into mountains and mountain-related activities such as skiing, hiking, and trail biking. This site is where I display some of the web work I am proud of.

My Background

I grew up in Colorado in the shadow of the Rocky Mountains. For college I studied political science at Eckerd College in St. Petersburg, Florida.

Upon graduating I became fascinated by the creativity and challenge of web development. I spent the next year traveling in Southeast Asia and Europe while building websites for clients.

Currently I am leveling up these skills at the intensive front-end development bootcamp Galvanize, in Boulder, Colorado.
