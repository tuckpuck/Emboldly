Background

After graduating with a B.A. in political science from Eckerd College I became captivated by the creativity and challenges of web development. I spent my next year traveling through Southeast Asia while building websites for clients and friends.

I decided to further pursue my passion and spent 6 months at full-stack bootcamp Galvanize. Here I worked with teams on full-stack web applications. In the process I learned to properly plan a project, design practical user experiences, and communicate effectively with clients.

Since graduating in 2017, I have been coding every day and am more confident than ever in my ability to leverage various technologies and come up with great results. I am back on the road and seeking web opportunities of all kinds.
