Web Services

Need a Website?

Let me help make your web vision come to reality! I specialize in front-end solutions for small businesses to convey their message seamlessly online. The sites are visually engaging, highly functional, and make your web presence stand out.

What services do I offer?

I can take you through each step in a website's creation: design, development, and deployment. I am also well-versed in performance, responsive design, security basics, and SEO.

What experience do I have?

I am a developer who thrives where creativity meets good business. I am passionate about this craft and I have built up a diverse skill set, allowing me to find the optimal solutions to your challenges. I can offer reliable, friendly service that you won't get with a larger company.

The Process

Planning

You provide details to me, including filling out a short questionnaire with your goals for the website. I write up my proposed solution, timeframe, and budget estimate. If everything looks good we sign a contract and begin.

Design

I create a visual representation of the website and its layout. We review and refine until it looks ready for development.

Development

The design is converted to be ready for the web. Code is written, content is added, features implemented, and the site is tested on multiple platforms and across browsers.

Deployment

The final review and release of the site. After making sure everything looks good, I give you information you need to take the final product. The website is hosted online and control is handed to you.

Pricing

Cost and timeframe depend on the scope of your project. Start a conversation and I will respond with more specific info and a price quote.
