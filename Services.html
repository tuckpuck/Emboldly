What can I do for you?

I can help build your next web project! I work closely with clients to make your vision come to reality. These sites are designed to be accessible, secure, and make your online presence stand out.

What experience do I have?

I am a front-end web developer who enjoys building a variety of websites and working with CMS platforms. I can build your personal, business, eCommerce, or CMS blogging website.

The Process

  • Planning phase - Initially, you provide details about your goals for the site. This will include filling out a questionnaire on your objectives. I write up a document with my proposed solution, a timeframe, and a budget estimate. If everything looks good we sign a contract and begin.
  • Design phase - I create a visual representation of the look and design of the website. We will review and make refinements until it looks good for development.
  • Development phase - The design is converted to be web-ready. Code is written, features are implemented, content is added, and the site is tested on multiple platforms.
  • Launch phase - This stage is the final review and release of the site. I give you any training and info you need. After making sure everything looks good, the website is hosted online and control is handed back to you.
