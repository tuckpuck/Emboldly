About Us

Penimex is Thailand's premier mangosteen distribution company. Freshly grown in the breathtaking tropics of Thailand, mangosteen are exotic, healthy, and delightfully juicy. They are harvested at dawn, sorted for quality and shipped carefully and quickly to our clients. Quality and customer satisfaction are our top priorities.

Johann Scheffer

Truly international German born, raised on an African farm, educated in the UK and lived in Asia since 1996 being a resident of Seoul, Hong Kong, Singapore & Bangkok. Fully committed to individual needs of multicultural customers over the globe, each mangosteen (queen of the fruit) is hand-selected to ensure top quality for connoisseur customers.

Colton Thomas

Associate Sales Director Colton Thomas worked on the sales team for LTA International (USA) for two years before joining Penimex. He has worked in South America, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East and has extensive knowledge in marketing and logistics.
